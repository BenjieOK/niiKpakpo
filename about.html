About Edgar Nii Kpakpo Addo

Practitioner, Engineer, Educator, and Entrepreneur

A Profile of Excellence and Dedication

Edgar Nii Kpakpo Addo is a distinguished Maritime Education Practitioner, Certified Engineer, and Entrepreneur, currently pursuing a PhD in Material Science and Engineering at the University of Ghana, Legon. As a marine engineering lecturer at the Regional Maritime University, he has established himself as a leading expert in maritime education and training.

Deeply committed to his faith and family, Nii Kpakpo’s personal convictions are anchored in Christian values. His mission is clear: to inspire a new generation of skilled professionals who will drive Ghana’s industrialization, close opportunity gaps, and unleash our nation’s full potential through the power of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET).

His consulting portfolio includes the design, fabrication, installation, supervision, and maintenance of engineering structures for prominent institutions like SMTC Global, Charkin Offshore Maritime, and Rig World Ghana.

"My mission is simple: make hands-on technical education the driving force of Ghana’s economic transformation—ensuring economic liberation through technical skill acquisition."

- Edgar Nii Kpakpo Addo
