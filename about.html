Edgar Nii Kpakpo Addo shares a comprehensive vision for leveraging Technical and Vocational Education and Training to drive industrialization and create sustainable jobs for the youth. The core of this vision is competency-based training that aligns directly with industry needs, ensuring graduates are not just certified, but are genuinely job-ready. "We must shift our focus from purely academic qualifications to practical, demonstrable skills," he argues. "That is the cornerstone of economic liberation for our nation."
